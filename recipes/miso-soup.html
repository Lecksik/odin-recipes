Miso Soup

Description

A famous cuisine from Japan that is especially easy to make, since it's only ingredients are miso, tofu, and water.

Ingredients

  • 2 green onions sliced
  • 1 sheet of nori cut into pieces
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp miso paste

Steps

  1. Heat up water in pot.
  2. Add tofu. Once cooked enough, add miso paste through strainer
  3. Add nori and green onions. Remove from heat.
